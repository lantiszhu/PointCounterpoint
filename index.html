    About

    Point Counterpoint is a 2-player arena shooter with a color-perspective theme.
    It was developed over a weekend for the Global Game Jam 2014.
    http://globalgamejam.org/2014/games/pointcounterpoint

    How-to Play

    Enemies of your color will chase you.
    You will only be able to shoot opposite color enemies and be hurt by same color enemies, forcing you to defend your ally.
    The goal of the game is to get the highest score possible by cooperating against the enemies.

    Controls

    We recommend playing with Xbox controllers. At least one Xbox controller is required.

    Xbox Keyboard
    Movement Left analog stick WASD
    Shooting Right analog stick Arrow keys
